Announcement for Internships 2025-2026

The School of Chemical and Biotechnology at SASTRA Deemed University offers a winter research internship and final year project internship for both undergraduate and postgraduate students. This program empowers aspirant research students to build a strong research foundation, encompassing essential skills such as hypothesis formulation, research design, problem-solving, research ethics, technical skills, scientific communication, interdisciplinary collaboration, and networking.

Total Intake: Only 50 seats available

Note: Choose your Research interest (listed below) and indicate priorities in the application

Internships Categories

Winter Research Internship

A short-term research internship

  • Duration: 4 to 6 weeks
  • Period: 15 November to 31 December, 2025
